WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Students, fresh builders, and multidisciplinary teams who want to prototype and pitch ideas.

CAN I JOIN SOLO?

Yes. Solo registrations are welcome, and team formation support will be available.

Check the registration page for current details. Workshops and selected sessions may be free.

No, but you should present a working prototype or a convincing proof of concept.
