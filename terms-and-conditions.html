01. WELCOME TO THE ARENA

Hey there! By joining NEXT-GEN HACK, you're basically saying "yes" to these guidelines. If you don't agree with them, that's totally fine - just know you won't be able to participate. Your involvement means you've read and accepted everything here.

02. WHO CAN PLAY

You need to be at least 18 years old, or have your parents/guardians give the thumbs up. Everyone must follow our code of conduct and local rules. If someone breaks these or acts inappropriately, NMIMS has the right to ask them to leave.

03. YOUR AWESOME PROJECTS

Your projects are yours! You own the intellectual property. When you submit, you're giving us permission to show off your work during the event and on our platforms. After the hackathon, all rights stay with you and your team.

04. STAYING SAFE

Hackathons can be intense, so we want you to be aware of the risks. We're not responsible for any injuries, lost items, or property damage. Please take care of yourself and your stuff during the event!

05. PHOTOS & VIDEOS

We love capturing the energy! By participating, you're okay with us taking photos and videos of you for event materials and promotion. If you'd rather not be in pictures, just let us know at check-in. We'll use them for educational and promotional stuff related to NEXT-GEN HACK.

06. WHEN YOU GET DISQUALIFIED

If someone cheats, copies work, breaks our rules, or uses banned stuff, they'll be disqualified right away. We reserve the right to remove anyone from the event for any reason we think is appropriate - no refunds in those cases.

07. WINNING & PRIZES

The judges decide who wins what. If you get a prize, you might need to help with some promotional activities. Prizes can't be traded or sold, and any taxes are on you. We want to be fair about this!

08. YOUR PERSONAL INFO

We collect some info when you register, and we only use it for running the event and keeping you updated. Your data stays private and follows privacy laws - we won't share it with others unless we absolutely need to for the event.

09. CHANGES TO THESE RULES

We might update these terms sometimes. If we make big changes, we'll let you know via email or our website. If you keep participating after that, it means you accept the new version.

10. QUESTIONS OR ISSUES

Got questions or problems with these terms? Reach out through our contact page. Everything here follows Indian law, and any disputes will be handled through the proper legal channels.
